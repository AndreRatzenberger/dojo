# T4 criteria

1. The response traces principles to concrete form, material, spacing, variation, repair, and use observations.
2. It distinguishes collection invariants from per-object variation and treats the tall cup as related variation rather than noise.
3. Cross-medium mappings explain operational functions such as hierarchy, restrained emphasis, honest recovery, and content primacy.
4. It explicitly rejects pottery colors, crack graphics, brush strokes, and craft language as literal copying.
5. It delivers implementation rules, freedoms, anti-patterns, a downstream prompt, and yes/no checks suitable for a service-status page.

Qualitative gate: the result should feel related through restraint and honest variation while remaining an accessible operational interface rather than a ceramic mood board.
