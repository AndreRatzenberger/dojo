1. The response analyzes syntax, cadence, diction, narrative distance, humor, omission, and paragraph movement using short coordinates from P1–P3.
2. It separates quoted evidence, observations, and inferred voice principles.
3. It explicitly excludes story facts, objects, imagery, distinctive phrases, and close sentence-shape imitation from transfer.

5. It supplies a standalone downstream prompt, degrees of freedom, anti-patterns, and yes/no originality checks.
