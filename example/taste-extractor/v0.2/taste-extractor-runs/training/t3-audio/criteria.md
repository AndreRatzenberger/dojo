# T3 criteria

1. Major principles trace to timestamps and distinguish direct listening notes from inference.
2. The translation preserves functions such as delayed response, rare convergence, shared anchor, visible recovery, role reversal, and quiet closure rather than copying sound.
3. The handoff specifies a runnable 30-minute sequence with roles, timing, exchange rules, convergence, disruption recovery, and ending.
4. It names invariants and adaptable choices and forbids literal music or performance theming.
5. It provides a downstream prompt and observable yes/no checks for the implemented meeting.

Qualitative gate: a facilitator should be able to run the meeting without inventing missing steps, while participants should not feel they are reenacting a composition.
