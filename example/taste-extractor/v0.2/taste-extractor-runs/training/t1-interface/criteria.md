# T1 criteria

1. Every major principle traces to a named surface or state, with observation separated from inference.
2. Recurring core surfaces outweigh the temporary conference screen; the outlier is not promoted into an invariant.
3. Source content, adaptable surface manifestations, and portable principles are distinguished.
4. The target handoff covers normal, success, error, and empty behavior and states that accessibility and operational clarity outrank style.
5. The result includes a thesis, invariants, degrees of freedom, anti-patterns, a downstream prompt, and yes/no validation checks.

Qualitative gate: the handoff must be usable by a panel designer without guessing what adjectives mean, and must not turn the panel into a campaign-themed imitation.
