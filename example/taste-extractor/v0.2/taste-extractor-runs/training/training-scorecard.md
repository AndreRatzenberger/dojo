# Training scorecard

One trial per case was predeclared. All qualitative gates passed; the binary
criteria expose the implementation gaps that polished prose alone concealed.

| Case | No-skill baseline | Final skilled | Change |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| T1 interface family across states | 2/5 | 5/5 | corrected |
| T2 prose voice to product copy | 0/5 | 5/5 | corrected after one rejected edit |
| T3 audio sequence to facilitation | 2/5 | 5/5 | corrected |
| T4 craft collection to status service | 3/5 | 5/5 | corrected |
| T5 service ritual to onboarding flow | 2/5 | 5/5 | corrected |
| T6 partial video evidence | 1/5 | 5/5 | corrected after one bounded edit |
| **Total** | **10/30** | **30/30** | **+20 criteria** |

The final full regression caught two late gaps that an earlier green run had
missed. T1 needed to state explicitly that accessibility, correctness, and
operational clarity outrank style. T2 needed every requested prose axis to carry
its own coordinate and source fragment. The T6 edit made partial-evidence
outputs explicitly provisional and bounded them to the modalities actually
inspected.

These six cases became training evidence. None was reused as a final holdout.
