# T5 criteria

1. The response traces principles to observed sequence, pacing, role, information, exception, and closure moments.
2. It separates portable service relationships from hotel content and decorative manifestations.
3. The target handoff covers first-time, returning, delayed-setup, recovery, and chosen-exit states.
4. It preserves low initial demand, progressive disclosure, direct uncertainty, promised follow-up, and non-promotional closure.
5. It provides a runnable production sequence, downstream prompt, anti-patterns, and yes/no state-transition checks.

Qualitative gate: the app flow should inherit calm agency and reliable recovery without using hospitality language or requiring a designer to invent missing states.
